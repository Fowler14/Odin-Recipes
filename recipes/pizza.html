Cheese Pizza

Description

Cheese pizza recipe with steps for homemade dough if you so choose.

This recipe is only three ingredients if you have pizza dough ready, or store bought dough.

Please enjoy this simple Cheese Pizza recipe.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Sauce - Homemade Pizza Sauce
  • Pizza Dough - Homemade Pizza Dough
  • 1 lb Shredded Mozzarella

Steps

  1. On a lightly floured surface, stretch the dough into a 12-inch circle, or desired size. Transfer to a non-stick baking sheet.
  2. Spread the pizza sauce onto the dough, leaving a 1-inch border around the edge.
  3. Sprinkle with mozzarella. Depending on how cheesy you want your pizza.
  4. Sprinkle with parmesan before baking for 12 to 14 minutes, or until the crust is browned and the cheese is bubbling.
